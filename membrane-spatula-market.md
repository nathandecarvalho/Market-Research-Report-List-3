<p><p>Membrane spatulas come in two main types: single-ended and double-ended. Single-ended membrane spatulas have a flat, narrow tip on one end, ideal for scraping or lifting thin membranes. On the other hand, double-ended membrane spatulas have two flat, narrow tips on each end, making them versatile for various laboratory tasks. Both types of membrane spatulas are commonly used in research, pharmaceutical, and chemical labs for handling delicate membranes and samples with precision and care.</p></p>
