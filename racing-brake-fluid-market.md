<p><p>Racing brake fluid comes in various types, with the most common being DOT4, DOT5, and DOT5.1. DOT4 is a glycol-based fluid suitable for high-performance vehicles, offering good performance and compatibility with most brake systems. DOT5 is silicone-based, known for its resistance to moisture absorption and high boiling point. DOT5.1 is a high-performance glycol-based fluid with superior boiling points and stability under extreme conditions, ideal for racing applications. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in the racing industry.</p></p>
