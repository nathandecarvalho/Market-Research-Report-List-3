<p><p>Dynamic Light Scattering and Laser Particle Size Analyzers are two types of instruments used to measure the size of nano particles. Dynamic Light Scattering works by analyzing the fluctuations in light scattered by particles in a sample, providing information on particle size distribution. Laser Particle Size Analyzers utilize laser diffraction to determine particle size distribution by measuring the intensity of light scattered by particles. Both methods are commonly used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and materials science for particle size analysis.</p></p>
