<p><p>The adhesive coatings market consists of various types such as non-reactive based adhesive, solvent based adhesive, water-based dispersion adhesive, and others. Non-reactive based adhesive coatings do not require any chemical reaction for bonding, while solvent based adhesive coatings use organic solvents for application. Water-based dispersion adhesive coatings use water as a carrier for dispersing resin and other ingredients. Other types of adhesive coatings include hot melt adhesives, pressure-sensitive adhesives, and radiation-curable adhesives. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in different industries.</p></p>
