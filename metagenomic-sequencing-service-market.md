<p><p>Metagenomic sequencing service market includes various types such as shotgun metagenomic sequencing, which involves sequencing all genetic material in a sample; 16S rRNA sequencing, which targets a specific gene to identify bacterial species; metatranscriptomics, which focuses on the activity of microbial communities; whole-genome sequencing, which sequences the entire genome of an organism; and de novo assembly, which involves piecing together genome sequences without a reference. Each type offers unique insights into microbial communities and can be used in various research applications.</p></p>
