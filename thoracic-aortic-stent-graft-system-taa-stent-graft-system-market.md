There are two main types of TAA Stent Graft Systems - Unibody and Bifurcated. Unibody stent grafts consist of a single piece design, while Bifurcated stent grafts have branches to extend into the iliac arteries. Unibody stent grafts are typically used for simpler cases, while Bifurcated stent grafts are used for more complex cases where the aneurysm involves the aortic arch or its branching vessels.</p></p>
