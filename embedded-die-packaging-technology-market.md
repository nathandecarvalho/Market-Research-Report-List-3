<p><p>Embedded Die Packaging Technology is a type of packaging where semiconductor chips are directly embedded into a rigid or flexible board. In Embedded Die in Rigid Board technology, the die is integrated into a rigid substrate, providing strong mechanical support and higher electrical performance. On the other hand, Embedded Die in Flexible Board technology involves embedding the die into a flexible substrate, allowing for more versatile and bendable designs. Both types offer compact and efficient solutions for miniaturizing electronic devices.</p></p>
