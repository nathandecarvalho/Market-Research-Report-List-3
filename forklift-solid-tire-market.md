<p><strong>The Forklift Solid Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cured-On Solid Tire</li><li>Pressed-On Solid Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Forklift solid tires come in two main types: cured-on solid tires and pressed-on solid tires. Cured-on solid tires are manufactured by curing rubber on a metal wheel, providing excellent durability and resistance to wear and tear. Pressed-on solid tires are made by pressing a rubber tire onto a wheel using a hydraulic press, offering a more cost-effective solution with easier installation. Both types of tires are popular in the forklift industry for their ability to withstand heavy loads and rough surfaces.</p></p>
