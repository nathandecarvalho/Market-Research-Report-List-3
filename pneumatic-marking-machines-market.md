<p><p>Pneumatic marking machines are classified into three main types based on their design and functionality. Hand-held pneumatic marking machines are portable and easy to use, ideal for marking on large or difficult-to-reach surfaces. Bench-top machines are stationary and provide higher precision and accuracy for marking on small or delicate objects. Integrated pneumatic marking machines are automated systems that can be seamlessly integrated into production lines for efficient and consistent marking on a large scale. Each type offers unique features to cater to varying marking needs in different industries.</p></p>
