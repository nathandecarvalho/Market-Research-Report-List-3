<p><p>Parameter hematology analyzers are classified into various types based on the technology used for blood cell analysis. Laser type analyzers use laser light to detect and analyze blood cells. Electrical impedance type analyzers measure changes in electrical conductivity as cells pass through a small aperture. Capacitive type analyzers use electrical capacitance to measure cell size and count. Other types of analyzers may utilize different technologies for blood cell analysis. Each type offers its unique advantages and applications in hematological testing.</p></p>
