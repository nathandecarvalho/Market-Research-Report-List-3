<p><ul><li>Plastic Spacers</li><li>Plastic/Metal Hybrid Spacers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible spacer market includes two main types: plastic spacers and plastic/metal hybrid spacers. Plastic spacers are made entirely of plastic and are lightweight and cost-effective. They are used in applications where conductivity is not a concern. Plastic/metal hybrid spacers combine the benefits of both materials, offering both strength and conductivity. These hybrid spacers are typically used in applications where durability and electrical conductivity are important, providing a versatile solution for a variety of industries.</p></p>
