<p><p>Melanoma Cancer Diagnostics market includes various types of tests like Fluorescent In Situ Hybridization (FISH) Tests, Comparative Genomic Hybridization (CGH) Tests, and Immunohistochemical (IHC) Tests. FISH tests detect genetic abnormalities, CGH tests analyze changes in DNA copy number, and IHC tests detect specific proteins in tissue samples. These tests help in the diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment of melanoma cancer by providing critical information about the genetic and molecular characteristics of the tumor.</p></p>
