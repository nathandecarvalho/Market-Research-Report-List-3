<p><strong>The Automotive Stereo Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Stereo Camera</li><li>Static Stereo Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive stereo camera market consists of dynamic stereo cameras and static stereo cameras. Dynamic stereo cameras have the capability to adjust to changing light conditions, distances, and speeds while capturing real-time images. These cameras are more advanced and provide better accuracy in depth perception. On the other hand, static stereo cameras are fixed and do not have the ability to adapt to changing conditions. They are more suitable for stationary applications where consistent depth perception is required. Both types have their own set of advantages and are used in various automotive applications.</p></p>
