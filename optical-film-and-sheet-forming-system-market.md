<p><p>Optical film and sheet forming systems are used to produce thin films and sheets for various applications. The contact market involves the use of rollers or molds to directly shape the film or sheet, while the contactless market utilizes methods like extrusion or casting to form the material without direct contact. Both types of systems have their own advantages and are used depending on the specific requirements of the application. Contact systems are typically used for high precision applications, while contactless systems are preferred for continuous production processes.</p></p>
