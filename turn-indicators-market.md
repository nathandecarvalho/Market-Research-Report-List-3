<p><strong>The Turn Indicators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Type</li><li>Mechanical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Turn indicators can be classified into two types in the market: electronic type and mechanical type. Electronic turn indicators use sensors and microprocessors to detect when a vehicle is turning and activate the corresponding signals. These indicators are more accurate and reliable but may be more expensive. On the other hand, mechanical turn indicators use traditional mechanical components such as levers and switches to control the turn signals. They are simpler and more cost-effective but may be less precise than electronic indicators.</p></p>
