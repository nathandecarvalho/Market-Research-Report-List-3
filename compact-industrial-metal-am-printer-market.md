<p><strong>The Compact Industrial Metal AM Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Bed Fusion (PBF)</li><li>Material Extrusion (ME)</li><li>Directed Energy Deposition (DED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compact Industrial Metal AM Printers are available in various market types, including Powder Bed Fusion (PBF), Material Extrusion (ME), and Directed Energy Deposition (DED). PBF printers use a powdered metal material that is selectively fused together by a laser or electron beam. ME printers extrude a metal filament through a nozzle to build up the object layer by layer. DED printers use a focused energy source to melt metal powder as it is deposited. Each type offers unique advantages and capabilities in metal additive manufacturing.</p></p>
