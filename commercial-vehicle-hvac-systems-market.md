<p><p>Commercial Vehicle HVAC Systems can be classified into two main types: Automatic HVAC Systems and Manual HVAC Systems. Automatic HVAC Systems are equipped with sensors that regulate the temperature inside the vehicle automatically, providing a convenient and comfortable environment for passengers. On the other hand, Manual HVAC Systems require manual adjustment of temperature and fan speed by the driver or occupants. Both types of systems cater to the varying needs and preferences of commercial vehicle users, ensuring a pleasant driving experience.</p></p>
