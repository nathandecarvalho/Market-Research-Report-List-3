<p><p>RF modulators are devices used to convert signals from baseband to RF frequencies for transmission. Different types of modulators cater to various modulation techniques. IQ modulators allow for complex modulation schemes, while QPSK modulators enable higher data rates. Bi-Phase modulators are used for binary phase shift keying, quadrature modulators support quadrature phase shift keying, and BPSK modulators are specifically designed for binary phase shift keying. Other types include frequency modulators, pulse modulators, and amplitude modulators, each serving specific communication needs.</p></p>
