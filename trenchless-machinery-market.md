<p><ul><li>Horizontal Directional Drilling Machine</li><li>Pipe Jacking Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trenchless machinery market consists of two main types - Horizontal Directional Drilling Machines and Pipe Jacking Machines. Horizontal Directional Drilling Machines are used to install underground pipes and conduits without the need for digging trenches. Pipe Jacking Machines are used for the installation of larger diameter pipes by pushing or jacking them into place underground. Both types of machinery are designed to minimize disruption to the surrounding environment and are commonly used in urban areas or under sensitive landscapes.</p></p>
