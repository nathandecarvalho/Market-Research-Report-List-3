<p><p>Veterinary telemedicine market types can be categorized into synchronous and asynchronous telemedicine. Synchronous telemedicine involves real-time communication between veterinarians and pet owners through video conferencing or phone calls. This allows for immediate diagnosis and treatment recommendations. On the other hand, asynchronous telemedicine involves the exchange of non-real-time data such as medical records, images, or messages, allowing for consultations to take place at a more convenient time without the need for both parties to be online simultaneously.</p></p>
