<p><p>Automated External Defibrillator (AED) market consists of two types: semi-automated and fully automated devices. Semi-automated AEDs require the user to manually analyze the heart rhythm and deliver a shock if necessary, while fully automated AEDs automatically analyze the heart rhythm and deliver a shock without requiring user intervention. Both types of AEDs are portable, user-friendly, and are designed to quickly and effectively treat sudden cardiac arrest. The market for both types of AEDs continues to grow as awareness of the importance of early defibrillation increases.</p></p>
