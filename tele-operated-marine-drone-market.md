<p><p>Tele-operated Marine Drones come in various types, with two popular ones being Monohull and Catamaran. Monohull drones are characterized by a single hull design, offering stability and efficiency in calm waters. On the other hand, Catamaran drones feature twin-hull design, providing enhanced stability and payload capacity, making them suitable for rougher waters and heavier tasks. Both types are commonly used for various marine applications such as research, surveying, search and rescue, and underwater inspections.</p></p>
