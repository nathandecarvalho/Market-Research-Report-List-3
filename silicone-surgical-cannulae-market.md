<p><p>Silicone Surgical Cannulae come in two main types: Curved Cannulae and Straight Cannulae. Curved Cannulae are designed with a bend in the shaft to provide better access to hard-to-reach areas during surgical procedures. They are commonly used in plastic surgery for facial contouring. On the other hand, Straight Cannulae are used for more general applications where a straight shaft is needed for precise and controlled delivery of fluids or medications. Both types play a crucial role in modern surgical practices.</p></p>
