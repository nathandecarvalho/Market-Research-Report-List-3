<p><p>The commercial vehicle air conditioner market consists of two main types: reciprocating type and rotary type. Reciprocating type air conditioners use a piston-driven system to compress and cool the refrigerant, while rotary type air conditioners use a rotary compressor to achieve the same cooling effect. Reciprocating type systems are typically more common in larger commercial vehicles, while rotary type systems are often found in smaller vehicles due to their compact size and efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in keeping commercial vehicle occupants comfortable during long journeys.</p></p>
