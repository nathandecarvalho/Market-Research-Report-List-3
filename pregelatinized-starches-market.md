<p><p>Pregelatinized starches are available in two main market types: powder and granules. Powder pregelatinized starches are finely ground and easy to dissolve in liquids, making them suitable for various food and pharmaceutical applications. On the other hand, granules are larger particles that provide texture and stability in food products, especially in baking and confectionery. Both powder and granules offer convenience and versatility in formulation, catering to different industry needs for thickening, binding, and improving texture.</p></p>
