<p><p>Fiber reinforced composite market includes two main types - thermoplastic composites and thermosetting composites. Thermoplastic composites are materials that can be heated and molded into shape multiple times without undergoing any chemical change, while thermosetting composites are materials that undergo a chemical reaction during the curing process, becoming permanently set into the desired shape. Each type offers distinct advantages and disadvantages depending on the specific application, leading to diverse uses in various industries including automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
