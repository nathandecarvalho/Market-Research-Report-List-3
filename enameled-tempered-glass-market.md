<p><p>Enameled tempered glass is available in two main types - nontransparent and semitransparent. Nontransparent enameled tempered glass has a solid color or pattern that completely blocks the view through the glass, offering privacy and aesthetic appeal. On the other hand, semitransparent enameled tempered glass allows some light to pass through while still providing privacy and a decorative finish. Both types are popular choices for architectural and interior design applications due to their durability, safety, and customization options.</p></p>
