<p><p>Ytterbium fiber market includes two main types: Single-Clad Fiber and Double-Clad Fiber. Single-Clad Fiber has one layer of cladding surrounding the ytterbium-doped core, providing a simpler structure suitable for low to moderate power applications. On the other hand, Double-Clad Fiber features an additional inner cladding layer designed to improve the fiber's efficiency for high-power applications by allowing for better heat dissipation and minimizing optical feedback. Both types cater to different power requirements and applications within the market.</p></p>
