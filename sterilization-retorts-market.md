<p><p>Sterilization retorts are used in the food processing industry to sterilize food products before packaging. There are two main types of sterilization retorts: static retorts and rotary retorts. Static retorts are stationary and typically used for smaller batch sizes, while rotary retorts are continuously rotating and are suitable for larger production volumes. Both types of retorts use high-pressure steam to achieve sterilization and ensure food safety. The choice between static and rotary retorts depends on production requirements and efficiency considerations.</p></p>
