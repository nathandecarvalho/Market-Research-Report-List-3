DSP hearing aids use advanced technology to analyze and process sound waves, providing a more accurate and customizable listening experience. On the other hand, simulated signal processing hearing aids mimic the way sound waves are processed in a natural hearing system. Both types aim to improve the wearer's hearing ability by amplifying and enhancing sounds in different ways.</p></p>
