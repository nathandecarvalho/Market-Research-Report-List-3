<p><p>Labelling and Coding Machinery Market consists of various types such as Self-adhesive or Pressure Sensitive Labellers, Shrink Sleeve Labellers, and Glue Based Labellers. Self-adhesive or Pressure Sensitive Labellers use adhesive labels that stick to products when pressure is applied. Shrink Sleeve Labellers apply labels that shrink to conform to the shape of the product. Glue Based Labellers use adhesive glue to stick labels to products. Each type of labeller offers unique advantages and is used in different industries based on specific labeling requirements.</p></p>
