<p><strong>The Crash Management Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Crash Management</li><li>Rear Crash Management</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front crash management systems focus on preventing or mitigating injuries in the event of a frontal collision. These systems typically include technologies such as adaptive cruise control, automatic emergency braking, and lane departure warning. On the other hand, rear crash management systems are designed to protect passengers in the event of a rear-end collision. These systems may include features such as rear automatic braking and rear cross-traffic alert to help minimize the impact of a collision from behind.</p></p>
