<p><p>Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm (AAA) Repair Devices market includes two main types - Synthetic Graft System and Endovascular Stent Graft System. Synthetic Graft System involves surgical repair of the aneurysm using a synthetic graft to replace the weakened part of the aorta. On the other hand, Endovascular Stent Graft System involves minimally invasive procedure where a stent graft is inserted inside the aorta to reinforce the weakened area and prevent rupture. Both systems offer effective treatment options for patients with AAA.</p></p>
