<p><p>Tuna canning machines come in various types to meet different packaging needs. Vacuum canning machines remove air from the packaging to extend the shelf life of the tuna. Protective gas canning machines replace the air with a gas like nitrogen to prevent oxidation and spoilage. Both types of machines help preserve the freshness and quality of the tuna during the canning process, catering to different preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
