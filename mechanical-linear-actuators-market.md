<p><p>Mechanical linear actuators are devices that convert rotary motion into linear motion. Screw actuators use a screw mechanism to move a load along the axis of the screw. Cam actuators utilize a cam mechanism to achieve linear motion. Wheel and axle actuators use a wheel and axle system to transfer rotational motion into linear motion. Each type of actuator offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications depending on factors such as load capacity and precision requirements.</p></p>
