<p><p>Nonwoven composites market types include meltblown, spunbonded, staples, and others. Meltblown composites are made by extruding melted polymer through small nozzles, creating microfibers that are then randomly laid to form a nonwoven sheet. Spunbonded composites involve spinning continuous filaments onto a conveyor belt before bonding them together. Staples composites are made by layering short fibers and then bonding them together. Other types of nonwoven composites may involve different manufacturing processes or materials.</p></p>
