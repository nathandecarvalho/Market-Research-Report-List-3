<p><p>Antibacterial coatings market can be classified into metallic coatings and non-metallic coatings. Non-metallic coatings, on the other hand, use materials like polymers and ceramics to achieve the same antibacterial effect. Both types of coatings are gaining popularity in various industries due to their ability to create hygienic and germ-resistant surfaces.</p></p>
