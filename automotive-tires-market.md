<p><p>Automotive tires market can be segmented into two main types - bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with crisscrossed layers of ply cords, providing good load carrying capacity but less flexibility and durability. Radial tires, on the other hand, have ply cords laid at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, offering better handling, fuel efficiency, and longer tread life. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different vehicles based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
