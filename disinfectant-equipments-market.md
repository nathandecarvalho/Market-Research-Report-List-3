<p><p>Disinfectant equipment market can be classified into two main types: reusable and non-reusable equipment. Non-reusable equipment refers to single-use items like disposable wipes, gloves, and masks. While reusable equipment offers cost savings in the long run, non-reusable equipment provides convenience and eliminates the need for sterilization.
