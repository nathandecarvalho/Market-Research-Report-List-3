<p><p>Flat belt conveyors are commonly divided into two main types: open belt conveyors and enclosed belt conveyors. Open belt conveyors have an exposed belt running on rollers or a flat surface, making them more cost-effective and easier to maintain. Enclosed belt conveyors, on the other hand, have a protective casing surrounding the belt, offering higher levels of safety and cleanliness. Both types are used in a variety of industries for the efficient transport of goods and materials.</p></p>
