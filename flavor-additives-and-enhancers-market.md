<p><p>Flavor additives are substances that are added to food and beverages to enhance their taste or aroma. They are typically natural or artificial ingredients such as sweeteners, colors, and preservatives. On the other hand, flavor enhancers are compounds that are used to intensify the existing flavors in a product. This can include ingredients like monosodium glutamate (MSG) or yeast extracts. Both flavor additives and enhancers play a crucial role in the food industry by improving the overall sensory experience of products.</p></p>
