<p><p>Enterprise Network Firewalls market offers three main types: On-Premises, Managed, and Hybrid. On-Premises firewalls are deployed and managed by the organization itself, providing full control over security policies. Managed firewalls are outsourced to a third-party provider for monitoring and management. Hybrid firewalls combine the benefits of both on-premises and managed solutions, offering a flexible and scalable approach to network security. Each type caters to different business needs and preferences in terms of control, management, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
