<p><strong>The Mobile Cardiac Telemetry Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lead Based Devices</li><li>Patch Based Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile Cardiac Telemetry Monitors Market includes Lead-Based Devices and Patch-Based Devices. Lead-Based Devices typically involve the use of electrodes attached to the patient's chest to continuously monitor heart activity, providing real-time data to healthcare providers. Patch-Based Devices, on the other hand, are small, discreet patches that adhere to the patient's chest and can wirelessly transmit heart rhythm data to a monitoring system. Both types of devices offer convenience and continuous monitoring for patients with cardiac conditions.</p></p>
