<p><ul><li>Fixed Frequency Air Conditioning</li><li>Inverter Air Conditioning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger railway air conditioning market offers two main types of systems: fixed frequency air conditioning and inverter air conditioning. Fixed frequency air conditioning systems operate at a constant speed, providing consistent cooling but consuming more energy. On the other hand, inverter air conditioning systems adjust the compressor speed based on the required cooling, resulting in energy savings and more precise temperature control. Both systems are widely used in passenger railway compartments to ensure a comfortable and pleasant journey experience.</p></p>
