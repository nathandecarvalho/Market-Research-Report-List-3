Vented Tumble Dryers expel hot air through a hose, requiring proper ventilation for installation. On the other hand, Condenser Tumble Dryers collect moisture from the air in a separate container that needs to be emptied periodically. Each type has its own advantages and considerations, catering to different needs and preferences in the commercial laundry industry.</p></p>
