<p><p>Filtering centrifuges are classified into two main types based on their orientation: horizontal centrifuges and vertical centrifuges.</p><p>Horizontal centrifuges have a cylindrical drum that rotates on a horizontal axis, allowing for effective separation of solids and liquids. These centrifuges are commonly used in industries such as chemical processing and wastewater treatment.</p><p>Vertical centrifuges, on the other hand, have a vertical rotating drum that separates liquids and solids through centrifugal force. They are often used in industries such as food processing and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
