<p><p>Automotive brake systems have evolved to include advanced technologies like Antilock Braking System (ABS), Traction Control System (TCS), Electronic Stability Control (ESC), and Electronic Brake-Force Distribution (EBD). ABS prevents wheels from locking during sudden braking, TCS helps maintain traction on slippery surfaces, ESC enhances stability by reducing skidding, and EBD ensures optimal distribution of braking force to all wheels. These systems work together to improve overall braking performance and ensure driver safety in various driving conditions.</p></p>
