<p><p>Polymeric insulators are commonly classified into four main types based on their design and usage. Polymeric post insulators are used for supporting overhead power lines and providing electrical insulation. Polymeric pin insulators are used in distribution lines to support conductors and provide insulation. Polymeric long rod insulators are used in high voltage transmission lines to maintain insulation and mechanical strength. Other types of polymeric insulators include suspension, cap & pin, and station post insulators, each serving specific functions in electrical systems.</p></p>
