<p><p>Vehicle alarm system market consists of various types such as central locking, biometric capture device, remote keyless entry, steering lock, alarm, and immobilizer. Central locking allows all doors to be locked or unlocked simultaneously. Biometric capture devices use fingerprints or facial recognition for access control. Remote keyless entry enables locking and unlocking the car from a distance. Steering lock prevents the steering wheel from moving. Alarm systems alert in case of unauthorized entry. Immobilizers prevent the engine from starting without the correct key.</p></p>
