<p><ul><li>Skeleton</li><li>Stranded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full dry optical cable market can be divided into two types: Skeleton and Stranded. Skeleton cables are designed with a central tube surrounded by fiber optic filaments, providing strength and protection while allowing for flexibility. On the other hand, Stranded cables consist of multiple smaller fiber optic strands bundled together, offering higher flexibility and ease of installation. Both types cater to different requirements in terms of strength, flexibility, and installation methods in the optical cable market.</p></p>
