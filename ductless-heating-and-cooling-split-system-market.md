<p><p>Ductless heating and cooling split systems are categorized into three main types: mini split systems, multi split systems, and variable refrigerant flow (VRF) systems. Mini split systems consist of one outdoor unit connected to one indoor unit, providing individual control over temperature in specific rooms. Multi split systems involve one outdoor unit connecting to multiple indoor units, offering zoned cooling and heating capabilities. VRF systems use variable refrigerant flow technology to provide precise control over temperature and energy efficiency in larger commercial buildings or residential complexes.</p></p>
