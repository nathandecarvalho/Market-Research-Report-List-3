<p><p>Over-the-counter pain medication market can be divided into three main types based on how they are administered - oral, topical, and parenteral. Oral medications are taken by mouth and include tablets, capsules, and liquids. Topical medications are applied directly to the skin, where they are absorbed to provide localized pain relief. Parenteral medications are injected or infused directly into the body, bypassing the digestive system for faster and often more potent pain relief. Each type serves different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
