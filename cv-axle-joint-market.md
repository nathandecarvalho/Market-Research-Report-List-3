<p><strong>The CV Axle Joint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outboard Joints</li><li>Inboard Joints</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CV axle joints are vital components in a vehicle's drivetrain system. The outboard joints are located at the ends of the CV axle shafts and connect them to the wheels, allowing for smooth rotation and power transfer. On the other hand, inboard joints are situated closer to the transmission or differential and help in transmitting power from the engine to the wheels. Both types of joints play a crucial role in ensuring efficient performance and functionality of a vehicle's drivetrain system.</p></p>
