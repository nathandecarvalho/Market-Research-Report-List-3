<p><p>Double Piston Screen Changers are used for plastic extrusion processing and are available in two main market types: Continuous and Discontinuous. Continuous screen changers allow for uninterrupted production by continuously switching between screens, ensuring a constant flow of material. Discontinuous screen changers require the extrusion process to be momentarily paused in order to switch screens, resulting in brief interruptions in production. Each market type has its own set of advantages and limitations, catering to different production needs and preferences.</p></p>
