<p><p>Paints & coatings additives market is segmented into various types including biocides, dispersants & wetting agents, stabilizers, defoamers, and dearaters. Biocides are used to prevent microbial growth, while dispersants & wetting agents help in the dispersion of particles. Stabilizers are added to improve the performance and durability of the paint. Defoamers are used to eliminate foam formation, and dearaters help in removing air bubbles. These additives play a crucial role in enhancing the quality and properties of paints and coatings.</p></p>
