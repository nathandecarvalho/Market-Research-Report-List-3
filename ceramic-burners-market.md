<p><p>Grid ceramic burners are designed with a grid-like structure for evenly distributing heat while cooking. These burners are ideal for use in commercial kitchens and high-demand cooking environments. Sleeve ceramic burners, on the other hand, are designed with a sleeve shape that provides a more concentrated and intense heat source. They are commonly used in industrial applications where high temperatures are required for processes such as melting or shaping materials. Both types of ceramic burners offer efficient and reliable heat sources for various applications.</p></p>
