<p><p>Hot melt adhesives are widely used in the medical industry for various applications due to their strong bonding capabilities and quick curing times. SBS hot melt adhesive, made from styrene-butadiene-styrene block copolymers, offers excellent flexibility and adhesion properties, making it ideal for wound dressings and medical tapes. SIS hot melt adhesive, composed of styrene-isoprene-styrene block copolymers, provides superior heat resistance and elasticity, making it suitable for applications like medical packaging and surgical drapes. Both types of hot melt adhesives play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and effectiveness of medical products.</p></p>
