<p><strong>The RFID Asset Tracking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active RFID Type</li><li>Passive RFID Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID Asset Tracking System market is divided into two types: Active RFID and Passive RFID. Active RFID systems use battery-powered tags that constantly broadcast their signal, allowing for longer read ranges and real-time tracking. Passive RFID systems, on the other hand, rely on readers to provide power to the tags, limiting their read range but making them more cost-effective for tracking larger volumes of assets. Both types offer unique advantages and are used in various industries for efficient asset management.</p></p>
