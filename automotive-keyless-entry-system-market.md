<p><p>Automotive Keyless Entry System market includes Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows the driver to unlock the car and start the engine without taking the key fob out of their pocket or bag. RKES, on the other hand, requires the driver to press a button on the key fob to unlock the car or start the engine. Both systems provide convenience and security benefits to users, driving the growth of the keyless entry system market in the automotive industry.</p></p>
