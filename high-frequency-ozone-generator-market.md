<p><p>High Frequency Ozone Generators can be categorized into two main types based on their cooling method: Water-cooled and Air-cooled models. Water-cooled ozone generators use water as a coolant to dissipate heat generated during the ozone production process, while air-cooled models use air for the same purpose. Water-cooled generators are known for their higher efficiency and ability to generate a larger amount of ozone, making them suitable for industrial applications. On the other hand, air-cooled generators are more compact and easier to install, making them ideal for smaller scale applications.</p></p>
