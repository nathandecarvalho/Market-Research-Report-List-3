<p><strong>The Dialysis Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dialysis services are mainly categorized into two types: Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis. Hemodialysis involves filtering a patient's blood through a machine to remove waste and excess fluids. This type of dialysis is usually done at a dialysis center by medical professionals. On the other hand, Peritoneal Dialysis involves using the patient's peritoneum (membrane lining the abdominal cavity) as a filter to remove waste and fluids. Patients can perform Peritoneal Dialysis at home, giving them more flexibility and independence in managing their treatment.</p></p>
