<p><p>Ball conveyors are a popular choice for material handling in various industries due to their efficiency and versatility. There are two main types of ball conveyors in the market: stationary type and extendible type. Stationary ball conveyors are fixed in one position and are ideal for continuous, high-volume transportation. Extendible ball conveyors, on the other hand, can be adjusted in length to accommodate varying sizes of materials or goods. Both types offer a reliable and efficient solution for moving items along assembly lines or within warehouses.</p></p>
