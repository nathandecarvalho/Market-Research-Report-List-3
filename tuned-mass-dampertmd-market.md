<p><p>A Tuned Mass Damper (TMD) is a device used in structures to reduce vibrations caused by external forces such as wind, earthquakes, or machinery. It consists of a mass attached to a spring and a damping element, designed to oscillate out of phase with the structure's natural frequency, thereby reducing the amplitude of vibrations. TMDs are commonly used in skyscrapers, bridges, and chimneys to improve structural stability and occupant comfort.</p><p>The Tuned Mass Damper (TMD) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period.
