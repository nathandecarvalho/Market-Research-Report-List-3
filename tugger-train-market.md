<p><p>Tugger trains can be classified into three main market types based on their functionality and design: Trailer style, Taxi style, and Push style. Trailer style tugger trains consist of a motorized tugger pulling multiple trailers loaded with materials. Taxi style tugger trains feature a platform that can carry multiple carts or pallets of material. Push style tugger trains involve a single operator pushing a train of carts or pallets. Each market type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in material handling and logistics operations.</p></p>
