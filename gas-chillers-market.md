<p><ul><li>Open Loop</li><li>Closed Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas chillers are commonly classified into Open Loop and Closed Loop markets based on their design and functionality. Open Loop gas chillers are systems that utilize gas, such as ammonia or carbon dioxide, to cool a liquid refrigerant by passing it through a series of heat exchangers. In comparison, Closed Loop gas chillers recirculate the same gas refrigerant through a closed circuit system, maintaining consistent cooling efficiency. Both types of gas chillers are essential in various industrial and commercial applications for maintaining proper temperature control.</p></p>
