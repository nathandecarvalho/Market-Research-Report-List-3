<p><p>Thermal cyclers are widely categorized into real-time thermal cyclers, gradient thermal cyclers, and other types. Real-time thermal cyclers allow continuous monitoring of the amplification process, providing real-time data on gene expression levels. Gradient thermal cyclers are equipped with multiple temperature zones, allowing for optimization of annealing temperatures across the sample. Other types of thermal cyclers include standard block thermal cyclers and multiplex PCR thermal cyclers. Each type offers specific features tailored to different research needs in molecular biology and diagnostics.</p></p>
