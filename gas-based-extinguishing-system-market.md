<p><p>Gas-based extinguishing systems are classified into various types based on the extinguishing agent used. Clean Agent Fire Suppression System utilizes non-water based agents to extinguish fires quickly without leaving behind residue. CO2 System works by displacing oxygen to smother the fire. Inert Gas System uses inert gases such as nitrogen or argon to reduce oxygen concentration to extinguish the fire. Other types of gas-based extinguishing systems include halocarbon-based agents and chemical agents that suppress fires by interrupting the chemical reaction.</p></p>
