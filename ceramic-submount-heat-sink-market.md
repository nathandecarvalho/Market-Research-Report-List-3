<p><ul><li>AIN Heat Sink</li><li>Alumina Heat Sink</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic submount heat sinks are categorized into different types based on the material used. AIN (aluminum nitride) heat sinks are known for their high thermal conductivity and insulation properties, making them ideal for high-power applications. Alumina heat sinks, on the other hand, offer excellent thermal stability and mechanical strength. The "others" category includes ceramic heat sinks made from various materials such as boron nitride or silicon nitride. Each type has unique characteristics that cater to specific heat dissipation requirements in various applications.</p></p>
