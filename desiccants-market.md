<p><strong>The Desiccants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Absorption</li><li>Chemical Absorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Desiccants come in two main types: Physical Absorption and Chemical Absorption. Physical Absorption desiccants work by physically attracting and holding water molecules through absorption. They are often made from materials like silica gel or activated carbon. Chemical Absorption desiccants react chemically with water to remove moisture from the air. They often contain substances like calcium chloride or molecular sieves. Both types of desiccants are used in various industries to control humidity levels and prevent damage to products during storage or transport.</p></p>
