<p><p>Laser ablation machines are commonly categorized into three main types: YAG laser type, CO2 laser type, and others. YAG laser type machines utilize a solid-state laser to deliver high-energy pulses for precision ablation processes. CO2 laser type machines, on the other hand, use a gas laser to generate a continuous beam for more efficient material removal. The "others" category includes various other types of laser technologies, such as fiber lasers or excimer lasers, which are also used for ablation applications in different industries.</p></p>
