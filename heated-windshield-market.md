<p><strong>The Heated Windshield Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tungsten Wire Heated</li><li>Conductive Layer Heated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heated Windshield market includes two main types of heating technologies: Tungsten Wire Heated and Conductive Layer Heated. Tungsten Wire Heated windshields use thin tungsten wires embedded in the glass to generate heat when an electric current passes through them. On the other hand, Conductive Layer Heated windshields have a special conductive layer that heats up when electricity is applied. Both technologies effectively prevent frost, ice, and fog from forming on the windshield, ensuring better visibility and safety while driving.</p></p>
