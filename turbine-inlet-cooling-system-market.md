<p><ul><li>Inlet Fogging</li><li>Chiller System</li><li>Evaporative Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Turbine Inlet Cooling Systems are used to increase the efficiency and output of gas turbines by reducing the inlet air temperature. There are three main types of turbine inlet cooling systems: Inlet Fogging, Chiller System, and Evaporative Cooling. Inlet Fogging involves spraying fine water droplets into the inlet air stream to cool it down. Chiller Systems use a refrigeration cycle to cool the inlet air. Evaporative Cooling systems use the evaporation of water to lower the temperature of the inlet air.</p></p>
