<p><p>Robotic milking machines can be categorized into two main types: semi-automatic milking machines and fully-automatic milking machines. Semi-automatic milking machines require partial human intervention in tasks such as attaching the milking cups, while fully-automatic milking machines completely automate the milking process without any need for human involvement. Both types of machines offer benefits such as increased efficiency, reduced labor costs, and improved milk quality. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs and preferences of the dairy farmer.</p></p>
