<p><p>Non-destructive coating thickness gauges utilize different types of technology for measuring coating thickness without damaging the material being tested. Magnetic and eddy current technology are commonly used for measuring non-conductive coatings on metal substrates. Ultrasonic technology is used for measuring coatings on non-metal substrates. X-ray technology is used for measuring coatings on dense or multi-layered materials. Other emerging technologies in the market include infrared and microwave, providing a wide range of options for non-destructive coating thickness measurement applications.</p></p>
