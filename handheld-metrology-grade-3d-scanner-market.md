<p><p>Handheld metrology-grade 3D scanners utilize either laser or structured light technology to capture highly accurate and detailed 3D measurements of objects. Laser 3D scanners project a laser beam onto the object and measure the reflection to create a 3D model. Structure light scanners use a patterned light to create a 3D representation. Both types are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, construction, and medical field for quality control, reverse engineering, and designing purposes.</p></p>
