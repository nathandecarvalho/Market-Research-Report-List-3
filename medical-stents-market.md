<p><p>Medical stents are small, expandable tubes used to treat narrow or weakened arteries in the body. They are commonly used in procedures such as angioplasty to help keep the arteries open and improve blood flow. Medical stents are made of metal mesh or fabric material and are inserted into the affected artery to provide support and prevent blockages.</p><p>The global medical stents market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 10.8% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, rising geriatric population, and advancements in stent technology. The market is also witnessing a trend towards the development of bioresorbable stents, which dissolve over time, reducing the long-term risks associated with traditional stents.</p><p>Other key trends in the medical stents market include the increasing adoption of drug-eluting stents, which release medication to prevent restenosis, and the growing demand for minimally invasive procedures. Additionally, improving healthcare infrastructure and rising healthcare expenditure in emerging markets are expected to further fuel market growth in the coming years.</p></p>
