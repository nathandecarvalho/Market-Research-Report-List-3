<p><ul><li>Air Anesthesia Machine</li><li>DC Anesthesia Machine</li><li>Closed Anesthesia Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anesthesia machinery market consists of three main types: Air Anesthesia Machine, DC Anesthesia Machine, and Closed Anesthesia Machine. Air Anesthesia Machine delivers gases such as oxygen and nitrous oxide directly to the patient. DC Anesthesia Machine uses a pneumatic system to control gas flow and pressure, allowing for precise anesthesia administration. Closed Anesthesia Machine recirculates exhaled gases, reducing waste and exposure to harmful substances. Each type provides distinct advantages and is tailored to meet specific needs of healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
