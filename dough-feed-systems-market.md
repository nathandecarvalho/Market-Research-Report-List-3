<p><p>Dough feed systems are used in bakery and food production to automate the process of feeding dough into machines for further processing. There are two main types of dough feed systems: automatic and semi-automatic. </p><p>Automatic feed systems require minimal human intervention as they are fully automated and can feed dough continuously without the need for manual input. On the other hand, semi-automatic feed systems involve some level of manual operation, such as placing dough in the feed system before it is automatically processed. Both types of systems offer efficiency and consistency in dough feeding for improved production processes.</p></p>
