<p><p>Melamine panels are commonly categorized into two main types - Particle Panels and Density Panels. Particle panels are made with wood particles bonded together with melamine resin, providing a cost-effective option for furniture and cabinetry. Density panels, on the other hand, are made with higher density fiberboard for increased strength and durability. Both types of melamine panels are widely used in the construction and furniture industries due to their versatile applications and aesthetically pleasing finishes.</p></p>
