The Automatic Locking Differential engages automatically when slip is detected, enhancing performance on various terrain. The Selectable Locking Differential allows drivers to manually engage the system when needed, offering more control. Spool locking differentials provide maximum traction but are mainly used in racing applications due to their full-time engagement. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of drivers in various situations.</p></p>
