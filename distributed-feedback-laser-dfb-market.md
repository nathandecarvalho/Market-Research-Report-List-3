<p><p>A Distributed Feedback Laser (DFB) is a type of laser diode that incorporates a periodic structure in the active region, which acts as a distributed feedback mechanism for the emitted light. This design allows for single-mode operation, narrow linewidth, and high output power, making DFB lasers popular for a wide range of applications such as telecommunications, fiber optics, spectroscopy, and sensing.</p><p>The Distributed Feedback Laser (DFB) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for high-speed data transmission, rise in adoption of internet of things (IoT) devices, and advancements in telecommunications infrastructure are driving the growth of the DFB laser market. Additionally, the growing use of DFB lasers in medical applications such as surgery, diagnostics, and therapy is expected to fuel market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Distributed Feedback Laser (DFB) Market include the development of tunable DFB lasers for wavelength agility, integration of DFB lasers in silicon photonics for improved performance, and increasing research and development activities to enhance the efficiency and reliability of DFB lasers. Overall, the DFB laser market is poised for significant growth in the coming years.</p></p>
