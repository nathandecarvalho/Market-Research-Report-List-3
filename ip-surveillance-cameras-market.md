<p><p>Centralized IP Surveillance Cameras are connected to a central network video recorder (NVR) which manages the recording, storage, and video management of multiple cameras. This type of system offers centralized control and monitoring capabilities. Decentralized IP Surveillance Cameras, on the other hand, operate independently without the need for a central NVR. Each camera has its own built-in recording and storage capabilities, allowing for a more flexible and scalable surveillance system. Both types cater to the growing demand for advanced security solutions in various industries.</p></p>
