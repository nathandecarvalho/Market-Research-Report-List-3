<p><p>Carburetor cleaners are available in two main types: chlorinated and non-chlorinated. However, they are also considered to be more toxic and harmful to the environment. Non-chlorinated cleaners, on the other hand, use less harmful solvents like acetone or isopropanol to clean carburetors. While they may be less aggressive in cleaning, they are safer to use and more environmentally friendly options.</p></p>
