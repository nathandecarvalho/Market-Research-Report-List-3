<p><p>On-Site Hydrogen Generator market consists of two main types - Traditional Alkaline Electroliser and PEM Electroliser. Traditional Alkaline Electroliser uses a liquid alkaline solution to produce hydrogen, while PEM Electroliser utilizes a solid polymer electrolyte membrane to split water into hydrogen and oxygen. Traditional Alkaline Electroliser is known for its cost-effectiveness and reliability, while PEM Electroliser is preferred for its efficiency, compact size, and ability to operate at lower temperatures. Both types cater to the growing demand for on-site hydrogen production in various industries.</p></p>
