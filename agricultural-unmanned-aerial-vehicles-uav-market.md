Fixed-wing UAVs are better suited for covering large areas efficiently and for longer flight times, making them ideal for mapping and surveying purposes. On the other hand, multi-rotor UAVs are more maneuverable and can hover in place, making them suitable for tasks that require precise positioning and close-range inspections. Both types of UAVs play an important role in modern agriculture by providing farmers with valuable data for decision-making.</p></p>
