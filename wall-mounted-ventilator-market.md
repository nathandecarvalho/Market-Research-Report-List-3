<p><p>Wall-mounted ventilators come in two main types - one-way flow type and bidirectional flow type. One-way flow type ventilators are designed to expel air in a single direction, typically from inside to outside. Bidirectional flow type ventilators, on the other hand, can intake and expel air in both directions, offering more flexibility in ventilation options. Both types of ventilators are commonly used in households, offices, and other indoor spaces to improve air quality and maintain proper ventilation.</p></p>
