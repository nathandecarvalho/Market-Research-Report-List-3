<p><p>Bulk Handling System Market is classified into Forklift Loading (FL) Type and Dedicated Hoist Load (DH) Type. FL Type systems use forklifts to load and unload materials from trucks or containers, offering flexibility and easy maneuverability. On the other hand, DH Type systems are equipped with dedicated hoists for efficient vertical transportation of materials, suitable for handling heavier loads and ensuring safety during the handling process. Both types cater to different industrial needs and offer distinct advantages in terms of handling efficiency and safety.</p></p>
