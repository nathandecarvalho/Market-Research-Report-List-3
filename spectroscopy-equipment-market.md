<p><p>Spectroscopy equipment market is categorized into three main types: Molecular Spectroscopy Equipment, Atomic Spectroscopy Equipment, and Mass Spectroscopy Equipment. Molecular spectroscopy equipment is used to study the interaction of molecules with electromagnetic radiation. Atomic spectroscopy equipment is used to analyze the elemental composition of samples. Mass spectroscopy equipment is used to determine the mass-to-charge ratio of ions in a sample. Each type of equipment serves a specific purpose in analyzing and identifying substances at a molecular level.</p></p>
