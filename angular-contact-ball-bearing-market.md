<p><p>Angular contact ball bearings come in various market types including 15°, 25°, 30°, 40°, and other markets. These designations indicate the contact angle between the balls and inner and outer raceways of the bearing. A smaller contact angle results in higher axial load capacity but lower radial load capacity, while a larger contact angle provides higher radial load capacity at the expense of axial load capacity. Different market types cater to specific application requirements, allowing for optimized performance in various industries.</p></p>
