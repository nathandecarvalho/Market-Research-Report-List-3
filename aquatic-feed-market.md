Mash feed: This type of feed is finely ground and mixed with water to form a thick paste before feeding the aquatic animals.</p><p>2. Pellet feed: Pellet feed is compressed feed in the form of small pellets, which are easy to handle and feed to the aquatic animals.</p><p>3. Expanded feed: Expanded feed is produced using high temperature and pressure, resulting in a highly digestible feed for aquatic animals.</p><p>4. Other market: This category includes specialized feeds such as extruded feed, crumble feed, and floating feed, designed for specific aquatic species and stages of growth.</p></p>
