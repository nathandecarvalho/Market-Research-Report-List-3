<p><p>HFFR (Halogen Free Flame Retardant) market can be categorized into three main types: Aluminum Hydroxide, Phosphorus-Based, and Others. Aluminum Hydroxide is a commonly used flame retardant that releases water vapor when exposed to heat, helping to extinguish flames. Phosphorus-Based flame retardants work by creating a char layer that insulates the material from heat. The "Others" category includes various types of halogen-free flame retardants such as magnesium hydroxide, boron compounds, and intumescent additives. Each type offers different flame retardant mechanisms to enhance fire safety in various applications.</p></p>
