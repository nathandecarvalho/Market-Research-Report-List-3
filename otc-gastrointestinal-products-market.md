<p><p>The OTC Gastrointestinal Products market consists of two main types - H2 Receptor Antagonists and Proton Pump Inhibitors (PPIs). H2 Receptor Antagonists work by blocking histamine to reduce the production of stomach acid, providing relief from heartburn and indigestion. Proton Pump Inhibitors, on the other hand, work by blocking the enzyme responsible for producing acid in the stomach, offering more effective and longer-lasting relief for conditions like acid reflux and ulcers. Both types are commonly used for the treatment of various gastrointestinal issues.</p></p>
