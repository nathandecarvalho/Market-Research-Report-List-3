<p><p>Marine bollards come in various types such as Tee Bollards, Horn Bollards, Cleat Bollards, Kidney Bollards, and Pillar Bollards. Tee Bollards have a T-shaped design for easy mooring. Horn Bollards have a curved shape for secure vessel attachment. Cleat Bollards have a cleat-like structure for tying off ropes. Kidney Bollards have a rounded shape for versatile mooring options. Pillar Bollards have a tall, vertical design for heavy-duty mooring operations. Each type of bollard offers unique features to meet different marine mooring requirements.</p></p>
