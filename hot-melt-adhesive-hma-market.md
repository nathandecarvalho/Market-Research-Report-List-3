<p><p>Hot Melt Adhesives (HMA) market includes various types such as Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA), Styrenic Block Copolymers (SBC), Metallocene Polyolefins (MPO), Amorphous Poly Alpha Olefins (APAO), Polyamides, Polyolefins, and Polyurethanes. EVA adhesives offer good flexibility and adhesion, while SBC adhesives provide high heat resistance. MPO adhesives have exceptional adhesion properties, while APAO adhesives offer low viscosity and good heat resistance. Polyamides are known for their strong bond strength, while polyolefins provide high-temperature resistance. Polyurethanes offer excellent resistance to chemicals and moisture in various applications.</p></p>
