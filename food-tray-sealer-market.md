<p><p>Food tray sealers come in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual tray sealers require the operator to manually place the tray and apply the seal. Semi-automatic tray sealers have some automated features, such as automatically placing the tray, but still require some manual labor. Fully automatic tray sealers are completely automated, with the machine handling all aspects of the sealing process. Each type offers varying levels of efficiency, throughput, and ease of use to cater to different production needs.</p></p>
