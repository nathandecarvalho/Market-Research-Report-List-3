<p><p>Water heating radiant ceiling panels can be categorized into two main types: top-mounted panels and wall-mounted panels. Top-mounted panels are installed on the ceiling and provide efficient heating by emitting infrared radiation downward towards the room. Wall-mounted panels are placed on walls and offer targeted heating for specific areas of a room. Both types of panels are energy-efficient, environmentally friendly, and provide a comfortable heating solution for residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
