<p><p>Oil-Less Rotary Vane Vacuum Pumps are classified into two main types based on their operational stages. Single Stage Rotary Vane Vacuum Pumps are designed with a single rotor and vanes, suitable for low to medium vacuum levels. Two Stage Rotary Vane Vacuum Pumps consist of two rotors and vanes, providing higher vacuum levels by compressing the gas in two stages. Both types are popular choices in industries where oil contamination is a concern, such as laboratories, medical devices, and food packaging.</p></p>
