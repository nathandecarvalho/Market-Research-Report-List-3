<p><p>Passenger vehicle air conditioner market can be categorized into two types: reciprocating type and rotary type. Reciprocating type air conditioners use a piston and cylinder arrangement to compress refrigerant gas, while rotary type air conditioners use a rotary compressor to achieve the same function. Both types have their own advantages and drawbacks, such as reciprocating type being more common and cost-effective, while rotary type being more efficient and compact. Each type appeals to different consumer preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
