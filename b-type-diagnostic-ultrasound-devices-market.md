<p><p>Fanshaped scanning, linear scanning, and mixed type are three different types of diagnostic ultrasound devices used in the medical field. Fanshaped scanning devices emit ultrasound waves in a fan-shaped pattern, making them ideal for obtaining images of large areas. Linear scanning devices emit waves in a linear pattern, allowing for detailed images of smaller, more specific areas. Mixed type devices combine both fan-shaped and linear scanning capabilities, offering a versatile option for healthcare providers needing to visualize various body structures.</p></p>
