<p><p>A fiber optical prism switch is a device used to control the direction of optical signals within fiber optic networks. There are two main types of fiber optic prism switches: Single Mode Fiber Switch and Multimode Fiber Switch. Single Mode Fiber Switches are designed for use with single mode fiber, which is capable of transmitting data over longer distances with higher bandwidth. Multimode Fiber Switches, on the other hand, are optimized for use with multimode fiber, which is better suited for shorter distance transmissions at lower bandwidths.</p></p>
