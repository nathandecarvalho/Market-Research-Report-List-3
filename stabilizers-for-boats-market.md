<p><p>Stabilizers for boats come in various types, with the two main market types being Fins Stabilizers and Gyroscopic Stabilizers. Fins stabilizers are fixed structures attached to the hull that reduce the roll of the boat in rough waters by creating resistance. On the other hand, gyroscopic stabilizers use spinning flywheels to counteract the boat's motion and provide stability. Both types of stabilizers aim to improve comfort and safety for passengers on board by reducing the rocking motion of the boat.</p></p>
