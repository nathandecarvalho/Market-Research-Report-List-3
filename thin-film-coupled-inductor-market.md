<p><p>Thin film coupled inductors are divided into two main types based on the core material used: low frequency core and high frequency core. Low frequency core coupled inductors are designed for applications where the operating frequency is below 1 MHz. They offer high inductance values and low losses at low frequencies. On the other hand, high frequency core coupled inductors are suitable for applications with frequencies above 1 MHz. They provide low core losses and high efficiency at high frequencies. These two types cater to different market needs based on the frequency of operation.</p></p>
