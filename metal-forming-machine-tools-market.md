<p><ul><li>Bulk Forming</li><li>Sheet Forming</li><li>Powder Metal Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal forming machine tools are categorized into three main types: Bulk forming, Sheet forming, and Powder metal forming. Bulk forming involves shaping metal materials into desired shapes through processes like forging, extrusion, and rolling. Sheet forming utilizes techniques such as bending, cutting, and deep drawing to create thin metal sheets into various shapes. Powder metal forming involves compacting metal powders into molds and then sintering them to produce final products. These different types cater to diverse manufacturing needs and applications within the metal forming industry.</p></p>
