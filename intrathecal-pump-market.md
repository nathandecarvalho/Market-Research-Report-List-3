<p><p>An intrathecal pump is a medical device that is surgically implanted under the skin to deliver medication directly into the spinal fluid. This method of drug delivery is used to manage chronic pain, spasticity, and other conditions that do not respond well to oral medications. Intrathecal pumps are filled with medication, and a catheter connected to the pump delivers the medication into the intrathecal space surrounding the spinal cord.</p><p>The Intrathecal Pump Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.9% during the forecast period. The growth can be attributed to the increasing prevalence of chronic pain and spasticity worldwide, as well as the growing awareness among healthcare professionals and patients about the benefits of intrathecal drug delivery.
