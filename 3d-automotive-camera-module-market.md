<p><ul><li>Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS)</li><li>Charge-Coupled Device (CCD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) and Charge-Coupled Device (CCD) are two types of 3D automotive camera modules. CMOS cameras are known for their low power consumption, faster readout speeds, and lower manufacturing costs, making them a popular choice for automotive applications. On the other hand, CCD cameras offer higher image quality, better low-light performance, and higher sensitivity to light. Both CMOS and CCD cameras play a crucial role in enhancing safety and convenience in modern vehicles by providing advanced driver assistance systems and improving overall driving experience.</p></p>
