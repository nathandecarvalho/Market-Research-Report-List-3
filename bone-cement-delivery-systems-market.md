High viscosity delivery systems are used for bones with good structural support, while medium viscosity systems are suitable for bones with compromised integrity. Low viscosity delivery systems are utilized for bones with poor structural support. The choice of delivery system depends on the specific needs of the patient and the surgical procedure being performed. Each type of system offers distinct advantages and is tailored to provide optimal results in different clinical scenarios.</p></p>
