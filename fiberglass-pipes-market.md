<p><p>Fiberglass pipes are categorized into different types based on the materials used in their construction. GRP (Glass Reinforced Plastic) pipes are composed of layers of glass fiber and resin, offering high corrosion resistance and strength. GRE (Glass Reinforced Epoxy) pipes are similar but use epoxy resin for added chemical resistance. Other types of fiberglass pipes may include those made with polyester resin or vinyl ester resin. These different types cater to various industry needs, providing durable and reliable solutions for a range of applications.</p></p>
