<p><ul><li>Self - propelled Wheel Type Full Feed Combine Harvester</li><li>Self - propelled Crawler Type Full Feed Combine Harvester</li><li>Self - propelled Crawler Semi - feed Combine Harvesters</li><li>Suspended Combine Harvester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Small combine harvesters are classified into four types based on their propulsion and feeding mechanisms. Self-propelled wheel type full-feed combine harvesters are equipped with wheels for mobility and can harvest crops in a single pass. Self-propelled crawler type full-feed combine harvesters have tracks for better maneuverability in rough terrains. Self-propelled crawler semi-feed combine harvesters have a combine mechanism that partially feeds the crop into the machine. Suspended combine harvesters are suspended from a tractor, allowing for easy attachment and operation in the field.</p></p>
