<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Air Purifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Electrical Charges Type</li><li>Positive Electrical Charges Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Negative electrical charges type automotive electric air purifiers utilize negatively charged ions to attract and remove airborne pollutants and particles from the air, such as dust, pollen, and pet dander. This type of purifier can effectively purify the air inside the vehicle, creating a cleaner and healthier environment for passengers. On the other hand, positive electrical charges type purifiers work by emitting positively charged ions that attach to and neutralize negatively charged pollutants, effectively removing them from the air. Both types of purifiers help improve air quality and create a more pleasant driving experience.</p></p>
