<p><strong>The Low Pressure Vacuum Carburizing Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Chamber</li><li>Double Chamber</li><li>Multi Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low pressure vacuum carburizing furnaces are available in three main types: single chamber, double chamber, and multi-chamber. In a single chamber furnace, the entire carburizing process takes place in one chamber. In a double chamber furnace, carburizing and quenching processes are carried out in separate chambers. Multi-chamber furnaces have multiple chambers for carburizing, quenching, and tempering processes, allowing for increased efficiency and flexibility in production. Each type of furnace offers different advantages depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
