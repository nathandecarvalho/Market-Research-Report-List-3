<p><p>Temperature and humidity loggers come in two main types: internal loggers and external loggers. Internal loggers are placed inside the monitored area, such as a storage room or laboratory, to track temperature and humidity levels. External loggers, on the other hand, are placed outside of the monitored area and connected to sensors inside. This allows for more accurate readings and better monitoring of conditions. Both types of loggers play a crucial role in maintaining proper environmental conditions for various industries such as healthcare, food storage, and manufacturing.</p></p>
