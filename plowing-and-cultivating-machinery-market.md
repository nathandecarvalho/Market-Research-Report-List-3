<p><p>Plowing Machinery: This type of machinery is used to break up and turn over the soil, preparing it for planting. It includes equipment such as moldboard plows, disc plows, and chisel plows.</p><p>Seeding Machinery: Seeding machinery is used to plant seeds in the prepared soil. This category includes seed drills, planters, and seed spreaders.</p><p>Harvesting Machinery: Harvesting machinery is used to gather crops once they are mature and ready for consumption. This includes equipment such as combine harvesters, forage harvesters, and cotton pickers.</p><p>Other Machinery: This category includes equipment that supports the farming process but may not fall directly into the plowing, seeding, or harvesting categories, such as irrigation systems, fertilizing equipment, and tractors.</p></p>
