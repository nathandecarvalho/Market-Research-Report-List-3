<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Embedded Pico Projectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DLP (Digital Light Processing) Pico Projector</li><li>LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) Pico Projector</li><li>Laser-Beam-Steering (LBS) Pico Projector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embedded Pico Projectors are small, lightweight projectors designed to be integrated into various devices such as smartphones, laptops, and cameras. The three main types of embedded pico projectors are DLP (Digital Light Processing), LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon), and Laser-Beam-Steering (LBS) projectors. DLP projectors use mirrors to reflect light onto the screen, LCoS projectors use liquid crystals on a silicon chip to project images, and LBS projectors use lasers and mirrors to steer light onto the screen. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses, catering to different market needs.</p></p>
