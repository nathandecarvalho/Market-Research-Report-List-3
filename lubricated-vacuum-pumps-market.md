<p><p>Lubricated vacuum pumps come in two main types: single stage and multi stage. Single stage pumps are simpler and generally used for applications where a lower level of vacuum is required. Multi stage pumps, on the other hand, are designed for more demanding applications that require a higher vacuum level. They achieve this by using multiple stages of compression to reach the desired vacuum level. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
