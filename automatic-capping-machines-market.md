<p><p>Automatic Capping Machines come in two main types: Fully Automatic and Semi-Automatic. Fully Automatic machines are designed to cap bottles without any human intervention, utilizing sensors and mechanical systems for high-speed and efficient processing. In contrast, Semi-Automatic machines require some manual involvement in placing the bottle under the capping head, while the capping process itself is automated. Both types cater to different production needs, with Fully Automatic machines ideal for large-scale manufacturing and Semi-Automatic machines suitable for smaller operations.</p></p>
