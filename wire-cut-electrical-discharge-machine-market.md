<p><ul><li>Wire-Cut Flushing Type</li><li>Wire-Cut Submerge Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire-Cut Electrical Discharge Machine market consists of two main types: Wire-Cut Flushing Type and Wire-Cut Submerge Type. The Wire-Cut Flushing Type machine uses a flushing medium, typically deionized water, to remove debris and maintain a consistent cutting environment. On the other hand, the Wire-Cut Submerge Type machine submerges the workpiece and cutting wire in a dielectric fluid to facilitate the discharge process. Both types offer high precision cutting capabilities for various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
