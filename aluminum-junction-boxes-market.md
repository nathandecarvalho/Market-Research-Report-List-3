<p><p>Aluminum junction boxes are available in various types such as IP66, IP54, IP67, IP55, and IP65, each offering different levels of protection against dust, water, and other environmental factors. IP66 junction boxes provide a high level of protection against dust and strong jets of water, while IP54 boxes offer protection against limited dust ingress and splashing water. IP67 boxes can be submerged in water for a short period, IP55 boxes provide protection against dust and low-pressure jets of water, and IP65 boxes offer protection against dust and low-pressure water jets.</p></p>
