<p><p>Residential and Commercial Swimming Pool Alarms market includes two main types: Fence/Wall Mounted Pool Alarms and Pool Mounted Infrared Detectors. Fence/Wall Mounted Pool Alarms are installed on the perimeter of the pool area and sound an alert when the gate or fence is opened. Pool Mounted Infrared Detectors are placed underwater and use infrared technology to detect any movement in the pool, triggering an alarm when unauthorized access is detected.Both types of alarms are essential for ensuring the safety of swimmers and preventing accidents.</p></p>
