<p><p>Construction core drill market can be divided into two main types based on the type of drill bits used - wet drill bits and dry drill bits. Wet drill bits are designed to be used with water or other cooling agents to prevent overheating and increase the lifespan of the drill bit. Dry drill bits, on the other hand, do not require any additional cooling agents and are suitable for use in dry drilling applications. Both types of drill bits cater to different construction needs and preferences.</p></p>
