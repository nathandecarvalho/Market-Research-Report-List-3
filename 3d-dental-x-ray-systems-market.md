<p><p>The ordinary X-ray machine is a traditional 2D dental imaging system that provides a flat image of the teeth and surrounding structures. It is commonly used for routine examinations and diagnosis of dental issues. In contrast, panoramic X-ray machines capture a single, flat image of the entire mouth in one shot, allowing dentists to view the entire oral cavity at once. This type of system is particularly useful for assessing the alignment of teeth, impacted teeth, and jaw structure.</p></p>
