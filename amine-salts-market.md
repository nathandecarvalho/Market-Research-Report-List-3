<p><ul><li>Diamine</li><li>Ternary Amine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amine salts are classified into two main types - diamine salts and ternary amine salts. Diamine salts contain two amino groups and are commonly used as curing agents in epoxy resins and as intermediates in pharmaceuticals and agrochemicals. Ternary amine salts, on the other hand, contain three amino groups and are frequently used as surfactants, emulsifiers, and catalysts in various industrial applications. Both types of amine salts play a crucial role in diverse industries due to their unique chemical properties and versatile applications.</p></p>
