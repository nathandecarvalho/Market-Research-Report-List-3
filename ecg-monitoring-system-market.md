<p><strong>The ECG Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Holter Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECG monitoring systems are categorized into three main types: resting ECG, stress ECG, and Holter monitors. Resting ECG systems are used to record the heart's electrical activity while the patient is at rest. Stress ECG systems monitor the heart during physical activity or stress tests. Holter monitors are wearable devices that continuously record the heart's activity over a period of time, typically 24-48 hours. Each type of ECG monitoring system plays a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various heart conditions.</p></p>
