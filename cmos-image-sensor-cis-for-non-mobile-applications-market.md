<p><p>CMOS Image Sensor (CIS) technology is widely used in various non-mobile applications such as security cameras, automotive cameras, and industrial machine vision systems. CIS can be categorized into two main types: Rolling Shutter and Global Shutter. Rolling Shutter CIS captures each frame line by line, which can result in distortion when capturing fast-moving objects. Global Shutter CIS captures the entire image at once, ensuring accurate representation of fast-moving objects. Both types cater to different market needs and applications within the non-mobile sector.</p></p>
