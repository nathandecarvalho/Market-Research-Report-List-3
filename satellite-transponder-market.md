<p><ul><li>C Band</li><li>Ku Band</li><li>Ka Band</li><li>K Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Satellite transponder market types are categorized based on frequency bands: C band, Ku band, Ka band, and K band. C band operates at lower frequencies and is commonly used for long-distance communications. Ku band offers higher frequencies and better signal quality for data transmission. Ka band offers even higher frequencies and is ideal for high-speed internet and multimedia applications. K band operates at extremely high frequencies and is used for specialized communication services. These different bands cater to various communication needs and offer different levels of performance and capabilities.</p></p>
