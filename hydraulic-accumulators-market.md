<p><p>Hydraulic accumulators are used to store energy and stabilize hydraulic systems. Three common types on the market are bladder hydraulic accumulators, piston hydraulic accumulators, and diaphragm hydraulic accumulators. Bladder accumulators use a flexible bladder to separate gas and fluid, piston accumulators use a moving piston to separate gas and fluid, and diaphragm accumulators use a flexible diaphragm to separate gas and fluid. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for specific applications in various industries.</p></p>
